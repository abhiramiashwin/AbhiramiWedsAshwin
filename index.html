Wedding Announcement



We are pleased to announce the wedding of

Abhirami N.P.
daughter of Smt. Reena N.V. and Late Shri. N.P. Satheeshan
WITH

Ashwin K.P.
son of Smt. T.P. Dakshayani and Shri. K.P. Prabhakaran
on

Monday, 24th August 2020
at
East Avenue Hotel, Nadakkavu, Kozhikode, Kerala

In order to adhere to the restrictions on social gatherings urged by the public health department, and also as a homage to the victims of the devastating Covid-19 pandemic, we have made a difficult choice to conduct the proceedings without the fanfare of a typical Indian wedding. This was a heartbreaking decision, but not nearly as heartbreaking as the idea of putting our loved ones at risk. We cordially seek your blessings and well-wishes for the bride-groom and also plead you to stay safe and well.

About Us



Bride's story

Abhirami was born as the first child of the two siblings in Kozhikode, Kerala and lived almost the entire part of her life in the God's own country. She did her schooling at St. Joseph's Anglo Indian Girls' High School, Kozhikode from Kindergarten till twelfth. Being very studious, she was the school topper and furthered her studies in medicine and obtained her Bachelors degree from Jubilee Mission Hospital in Thrissur. Due to her innate drive for education, she decided to pursue a Masters Degree after college, to which she is currently preparing. She had a brief stint at Meitra tertiary care hospital in Kozhikode where she served in their emergency department as a junior doctor.

A highly compassionate doctor at heart, Abhirami is also a naturally gifted singer and was trained in Classical music from age 6. She was tutored by eminent musicians in the city and always respected the art with sincere appreciation. She has won several accolades in music at district and state level competitions during her school days.



Groom's story

Ashwin was born as the second child of three boys and grew up in the family home in Kozhikode, Kerala. He completed his bachelor's degree in Mechanical engineering from Cochin University and started his professional life in Thane as a Design Engineer. While working, his passion for learning re-kindled and he proceeded to obtain a Masters degree and eventually a Ph.D. from BITS Pilani University and the Indian Institute of Science respectively. After his doctoral studies, he joined a team of young entrepreneurs in their quest to develop technologically advanced aerial drones.

Being a trained guitarist, Ashwin kept his passion for music alive even during his pursuit for higher education, and occasionally performed with different music groups. He is also a beloved uncle to his niblings and takes serious interest in keeping them intellectually and artistically engaged.

Our Family



Abhirami's family is based in Kozhikode district of Kerala and is the first child to her father Satheeshan N.P., from Nalukudipparambil, Kozhikode, and mother Reena N.V, from Valiyapurayil, Koyilandi. The family suffered a devastating loss in the year 2010 from the untimely demise of Abhirami's father who was a Marine Engineer. Her younger brother, a Chemical engineer graduated from NIT-Calicut is now working as a Senior Media Creative at Byju's in Bangalore. Abhirami's mother is a house-wife.

Ashwin's family has its roots at Kizhakkepurakkal in Mannur-Kozhikode, through their father who is a businessman turned planter and Thekkeppurakkal in Kallai-Kozhikode through his mother who is a retired employee of BSNL. His elder brother is an engineer settled in Dubai with his family of wife and two children. His younger brother is a lawyer practising at Calicut district court as well as Kerala High court. He is also a professional musician and is settled in Kozhikode with his wife and a child. The family is blessed to have their paternal grandmother living with them.
